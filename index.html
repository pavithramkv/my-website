GOLDEN PRIMARY SCHOOL

Admission open!

The Golden Primary School is a nurturing learning environment dedicated to building strong educational foundations for young children. With a focus on early childhood development, the school offers classes from L.K.G to primary grades, combining academics with values, discipline, and creativity. The school creates a friendly and secure atmosphere where children can grow confidently. Teachers are supportive, and they encourage students to learn through play, storytelling, and hands-on activities. Alongside regular subjects, children are introduced to art, music, and other co-curricular experiences that make learning joyful.

L.K.G to 6th Standard admissions are available
